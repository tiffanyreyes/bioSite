Hobbies

Music

Miguel is an avid music lover. When it comes to music, He loves listening to it and creating it. He prides himself on his vast CD collection that has many different genres that represent different eras or memories throughout his life. As for music creation, he first started when he formed a band with his high school friends and then it progressed as a solo hobby. He loves creating music with his guitar and piano. He built a home studio and learned to record himself, but he doesn't have the courage to publish anything seriously yet. He first learned ProTools but ever since switching to Mac, he enjoys making music in Logic Pro. His top bands are Foo Fighters and Paramore. He also has a strong appreciation for artists who use 70's or 80's influence into their songs and appreciates the craft in creating a radio pop hit.

Theme Parks

He is an avid theme park lover and has traveled to theme parks around the US. His favorite thus far is Disneyland in California because of the mixture of fun but with the food influence of LA. Roller coasters are also something he does not shy away from, but his least favorites are kid roller coasters because of the small clearances like in Goofy's Sky School.

Travel

He loves traveling. He used to move around a lot so he enjoys traveling to new places. Most of his travels have been local but he hopes to travel globally soon. He will say though his favorite trips are those he takes to the west coast. Having been on both coasts, Miguel can confidently say In-N-Out Burger is the best burger chain.
