About Me

Hello, this is Miguel Vargas. He is a Florida based full-stack developer who has 5 years of experience in the industry. He graduated from the University of Central Florida with a degree in Computer Science and prides himself for his passion for development. He enjoys the challenges programming allots and appreciates any chance to learn new things to help improve himself as a developer.

Miguel's main driver in life is his passion to constantly make improvements to things. If there is an opportunity to make something simpler or overall better for the user, He jumps at the chance to take part in it. It is the combination of making the solution and having it make a difference at a large scale which motivates him further when my work is published and praised. He has worked in a variety of industries from financial technologies, healthcare technologies, and theme parks/hospitality.

His interests closely align with the theme park industry since it mixes both work and leisure. One of his favorite projects was creating a theme park entry system from scratch on Android, which is his favorite mobile programming platform. Outside of coding, he enjoys playing video games like Halo or Rocket League and visiting the theme parks with his family. Though he enjoys his other hobbies in his free time, Miguel is still an avid programmer. He likes creating his own personal projects, one being his application on the Google Play store for WoW (World of Warcraft) Token Price. WoW Token Price was an app that enabled users to check the in-game currency value against 30-day game time.
